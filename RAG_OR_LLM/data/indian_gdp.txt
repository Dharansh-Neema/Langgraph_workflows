India's GDP is projected to reach approximately $4.3 trillion by the end of 2025, marking a significant doubling from $2.1 trillion in 2015, according to multiple sources. 
Here's a more detailed breakdown:
Projected GDP: India's GDP is expected to reach around $4.3 trillion by 2025. 
Growth: This represents a substantial increase from the $2.1 trillion GDP in 2015, marking a growth of over 100% in a decade. 
Global Ranking: India is projected to become the fifth-largest economy globally by 2025, according to IMF data. 
Overtaking Japan: India is expected to surpass Japan's GDP by 2025. 
Real GDP growth: India's real GDP is expected to grow at 6.5% in 2025. 
Inflation rate: The average consumer price inflation is expected to be 4.1% in 2025.
